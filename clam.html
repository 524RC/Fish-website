5 Facts about Clams

    1. Clams have no eyes, no nose, no ears, so they can't see, hear, or smell
    2. Clams are made up of a pair of shells and soft inner body
    3. Clams have no head,no biting mouth parts, and no arms
    4. Clams have one foot
    5. Clams have gills
